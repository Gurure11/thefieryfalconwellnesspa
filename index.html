Our Signature Massages

Swedish Massage

Gentle, flowing strokes to relax the entire body. Improves circulation, eases muscle tension, and promotes deep relaxation — ideal for first-timers or stress relief.

Thai Massage

Ancient healing combining acupressure, assisted stretching, and rhythmic compression. Increases flexibility and balances energy pathways without oils.

Hot Stone Massage

Heated, smooth basalt stones placed on key points and used to massage muscles. The deep warmth melts away tension, soothes aches, and improves blood flow.

Aromatherapy Massage

Swedish massage blended with customized essential oils to target emotional or physical states — from lavender calm to eucalyptus energy boost.

Deep Tissue Massage

Focuses on deeper muscle layers and fascia with slow, forceful strokes. Breaks down chronic knots, relieves persistent pain, and improves mobility.

Trigger Point Therapy

Isolated pressure on hyper-irritable spots (trigger points) within taut muscle bands. Releases referred pain patterns and restores muscle function.
